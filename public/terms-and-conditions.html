COMMISSIONS

I always welcome commission enquires here at Whitman Innovations. Whether working side by side with interior designers or directly with the client I’m happy to bring your vision to life.

THE PROCESS
The commissioning process usually starts with a email (or fill out the form below) and then an informal chat about your needs, ideas, budget and timescale.

‍DESIGN & QUOTE
‍Once I have a clear understanding of your brief, I’ll then develop a design for you to consider and give an estimate of the costs.

Once you decide on a design you like, I can give an acurate quote and we can agree on a payment schedule based on the size of the project. Typically the first payment is needed before the purchasing of materials and the last payment being needed before delivery.

‍MAKING YOUR PIECE
‍Finally, the vision comes to life. I always try share images of my work and my processes on social media so you will be able to see the progress of your piece through Instagram. I often send photo’s directly to you so you can see the progress first hand. At the moment I don’t allow any visitors to the workshop, but hopefully in the future this will be an option. So you can come and see in person how everything is made.

Items will remain the property of Whitman Innovations interior and decor , until full payment has been received.No cash refunds will be given; only in-store credit is provided. All our items are manufactured locally All wood is natural and not all grains and color are identical.

‍DELIVERY
‍I have a trusted courier that will deliver the finished piece to you to make sure it gets there safe and sound. There is a seperate cost to this which will is payable before delivery.

I look forward to making the process an exciting and rewarding one.
